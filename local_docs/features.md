# Features List

This document outlines all features available in the tutaller.com.ar web application, divided by user type. This list will be used to create detailed tutorials and blog articles for each feature.

---

## Student/User Features

### Account Management

#### **User Registration (Crear Cuenta)**
Allows new users to create an account by providing personal information including name, last name, email, DNI, birth date, and password.

**Value Proposition:** Enables students to join the workshop and start booking classes independently without administrative intervention.

#### **Login**
Secure authentication system using email and password credentials.

**Value Proposition:** Provides secure access to personal dashboard and booking features while protecting student information and booking history.

#### **Password Recovery (Olvidé mi Contraseña)**
Self-service password reset functionality that sends recovery instructions to the user's registered email.

**Value Proposition:** Reduces dependency on administrators for account access issues and provides students with immediate solutions to forgotten passwords.

#### **Profile Management (Perfil)**
Comprehensive profile section where students can view and update personal information, check payment status, and monitor credit balance.

**Value Proposition:** Empowers students to maintain accurate information, track payments, and understand their credit availability for booking classes.

### Class Booking & Management

#### **Class Booking (Reservar)**
Interface to view available appointment slots and book classes using available credits.

**Value Proposition:** Allows students to self-schedule their workshop attendance at their convenience without phone calls or administrative assistance.

#### **View Available Classes (Ver Clases Disponibles)**
Browse all available class slots with time, date, and capacity information.

**Value Proposition:** Provides transparency in class availability and helps students plan their schedules effectively.

#### **My Classes (Mis Clases)**
Personal calendar showing all booked classes, past attendance history, and upcoming reservations.

**Value Proposition:** Helps students track their workshop participation, maintain attendance records, and manage their schedule.

#### **Subscription Management (Suscripción)**
View and manage subscription plans, payment status, and subscription benefits.

**Value Proposition:** Provides clear visibility into membership status, renewal dates, and associated benefits, ensuring students understand their plan.

### Communication

#### **Notifications (Notificaciones)**
Centralized notification center displaying important messages from administrators, class confirmations, and system updates.

**Value Proposition:** Keeps students informed about class changes, workshop updates, cancellations, and important announcements without email overload.

#### **Receive Admin Notifications**
Get targeted messages from workshop administrators about promotions, schedule changes, or important updates.

**Value Proposition:** Ensures students stay informed about relevant workshop information and don't miss important announcements.

### Credits & Payments

#### **Credit Balance Display**
Real-time view of available class credits and credit usage history.

**Value Proposition:** Provides transparency in credit availability, helping students plan their bookings and understand when they need to purchase more credits.

#### **Payment Status Tracking**
Monitor payment history and current payment obligations.

**Value Proposition:** Helps students stay on top of their financial commitments and avoid service interruptions.

## Administrator Features

### Dashboard & Overview

#### **Admin Dashboard**
Central hub providing an overview of workshop operations, quick access to all administrative functions, and key metrics.

**Value Proposition:** Streamlines daily operations by providing single-point access to all management tools and current workshop status.

### Class Management

#### **Recurrent Class Management (Recurrentes)**
Create, edit, and manage classes that repeat on a regular schedule (weekly, daily, monthly patterns).

**Value Proposition:** Simplifies scheduling by allowing one-time setup for regular classes instead of creating each occurrence manually, saving hours of administrative work.

#### **Non-Recurrent Class Management (No Recurrentes)**
Manage one-time classes, special events, or workshops that don't follow a regular pattern.

**Value Proposition:** Provides flexibility to offer special sessions, workshops, or events without disrupting the regular class schedule.

#### **Reserved Appointments Calendar (Turnos Reservados)**
Interactive calendar view showing all booked appointments with filtering options by date, class type, or student.

**Value Proposition:** Provides visual overview of workshop utilization, helps identify busy/slow periods, and facilitates efficient resource planning.

### User Management

#### **User and Credit Management (Usuarios y Créditos)**
Comprehensive user database with ability to view student details, search users, and manage credit balances.

**Value Proposition:** Centralizes student information management, enabling quick credit adjustments, user lookups, and account management without separate systems.

#### **User Impersonation (Impersonar Usuario)**
View the application from any student's perspective without knowing their password.

**Value Proposition:** Essential troubleshooting tool to understand user issues, verify booking problems, and provide better support by seeing exactly what students see.

#### **Add/Remove Credits**
Manually adjust student credit balances for purchases, refunds, or special cases.

**Value Proposition:** Provides flexibility to handle special situations, promotional credits, or corrections without complex workarounds.

#### **User Search and Filtering**
Quick search and filter capabilities to find specific students or groups of students.

**Value Proposition:** Saves time when managing large student bases, enabling efficient user lookup and bulk operations.

### Communication Tools

#### **Send Notifications (Enviar Notificación)**
Create and send custom notifications to all users or selected user groups with custom titles and messages.

**Value Proposition:** Enables targeted communication about schedule changes, promotions, or important updates without relying on external email systems.

#### **Notification History**
View history of sent notifications and delivery status.

**Value Proposition:** Helps track communication with students and ensures messages were delivered successfully.

#### **Notifications Dashboard**
Bell icon dropdown showing recent class confirmations and system notifications for administrators.

**Value Proposition:** Keeps administrators informed of booking activity and system events in real-time.

### Configuration & Settings

#### **Space Configuration (Configuración)**
Comprehensive settings panel including:
- **Color Customization:** Adjust brand colors and visual theme
- **Space Information:** Update workshop name, description, and details
- **Subscription Settings:** Configure subscription plans and pricing
- **Student Permissions:** Toggle settings like allowing students to use credits for recovery or managing subscription access without payment
- **Booking Rules:** Set booking policies, cancellation rules, and capacity limits
- **Payment Integration:** Configure payment methods and financial settings

**Value Proposition:** Customizes the platform to match the workshop's brand, operational policies, and business model without requiring technical expertise.

### Analytics & Reporting

#### **Metrics and Analytics (Métricas)**
Comprehensive analytics dashboard tracking:

**Daily Metrics:**
- Number of bookings made
- Credits used
- Notifications sent
- Payments received
- Active students
- Slot utilization percentage
- Slot availability alerts

**Monthly Metrics:**
- Total monthly bookings
- Monthly revenue
- Student retention
- Class attendance rates
- Popular class times

**Value Proposition:** Provides data-driven insights for business decisions, helps identify trends, measures workshop performance, and spots potential issues before they become problems.

#### **Slot Utilization Tracking**
Monitor how efficiently class slots are being filled.

**Value Proposition:** Identifies underperforming time slots that could be eliminated or adjusted, and popular times that might need additional capacity.

#### **Student Activity Monitoring**
Track active student counts and engagement patterns.

**Value Proposition:** Helps understand student retention, identify at-risk students who haven't booked recently, and measure marketing effectiveness.

### Financial Management

#### **Payment Tracking**
Monitor all payments, track outstanding balances, and view payment history.

**Value Proposition:** Maintains financial oversight, ensures revenue tracking, and identifies payment issues quickly.

#### **Subscription Management**
View and manage student subscriptions, renewal dates, and subscription statuses.

**Value Proposition:** Ensures continuous revenue stream by tracking subscription renewals and identifying lapsed subscriptions.

## Shared Features

### **Responsive Design**
Both student and admin interfaces work seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

**Value Proposition:** Enables workshop management and class booking from any device, anywhere, increasing accessibility and convenience.

### **Real-time Updates**
Changes made by administrators (new classes, notifications) appear immediately for students.

**Value Proposition:** Ensures everyone has current information, reducing confusion and booking conflicts.

### **Secure Authentication**
Protected login system with password encryption and session management.

**Value Proposition:** Safeguards sensitive student information and ensures only authorized users access the system.
